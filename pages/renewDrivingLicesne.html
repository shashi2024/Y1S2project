Renew Driving License

Renew your License today.




  • Applicant should be present in person.
  • Applicant should be a owner of a driving license.
  • No need of redo the exams.
  • Need to submit original copies of these documents.
    • Copy of National Identy Card.
    • Birth certificate.
    • Medical certificate.
    • Black and white photograph of the applicant.
    • Copy of old License.


  • Fill the application given bellow with correct details.
  • Make sure to submit all the documents.
  • Do the payment. You are provided with both online payment method and offline payment method. Amount you should pay for update the license is Rs.2200.00.
  • License holders who have expired license may need to do additional payments.
  • If you fullfill all above requiremets you can renew your license.
